Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average porta potty rental cost?

The porta potty rental cost can vary based on the type of unit you choose, the duration of the rental, and the frequency of service. Our standard units are the most budget-friendly. For a precise quote on porta potty rental prices, please contact us directly.

Where can you place the portable toilet on my property?

We need a level surface and clear access for our delivery truck. We will work with you to find a location that is both convenient for your guests or workers and accessible for our service vehicles.

What is my responsibility when I get a porta potty on rent?

Your main responsibility is to ensure the unit is placed in a location where our service trucks can access it for drop-off, pickup, and servicing. You are also responsible for any damage to the unit beyond normal wear and tear during your rental period.

Can your restroom trailer rentals be used as temporary offices?

While our luxury portable restrooms are designed for sanitation, the high-end, climate-controlled interiors of our larger restroom trailer models have been used by clients as clean, private spaces for on-site managers in conjunction with their primary office trailers.

Does a restroom rental trailer require on-site staff?

For high-end galas and events, we highly recommend and can provide a uniformed attendant. The attendant keeps the portable restrooms for weddings or corporate events impeccably clean, restocks supplies, and ensures everything runs smoothly for your guests.

What is the recommended guest-to-unit ratio for a corporate event?

For a corporate event, we recommend a more generous ratio than for a casual festival, typically one flushing toilet stall for every 35-40 guests to minimize wait times and ensure a comfortable experience.
